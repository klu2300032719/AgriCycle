# AgriCycle — Investor One-Pager (Seed · $1M)

## The company
**AgriCycle** is the exchange layer for agricultural biomass. Farmers list crop residue, husk, stems, manure, and bagasse; industries buy graded feedstock; logistics and settlement close the loop.

## Problem
India produces **~350–500 million tonnes** of agri-residue yearly. Much is burned or sold through opaque brokers. Mills need reliable quality; farmers need buyers and fair prices. Stubble burning creates political and health urgency.

## Solution
A full-stack marketplace: list → AI price guidance → match → offer → escrow → transport → analytics. Built on Next.js, Neon Postgres, Better Auth, and Groq AI.

## Market
- India biomass market ~**$2.5–3.5B** (2024 estimates, growing)
- Platform model: **3–8% take rate** on GMV + logistics, financing, carbon later
- Beachhead: dense crop corridors, not “all of India day one”

## Product status
